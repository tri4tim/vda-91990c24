# Val d'Aran Ultra Trail Training Plan

**Race:** Mountain ultra trail, 5000m D+, July 1, 2026
**Base:** Leuven, Belgium (flat terrain)
**Constraints:** 6-12h/week, max 90km/week, max 2 strength sessions, Tue+Fri preferred gym days

---

## Weekly Structure Logic

- **Mon:** Easy run (lunch or evening)
- **Tue:** Gym — strength + moderate treadmill incline (evening)
- **Wed:** Track session with local team (evening, ~18:30-20:00) — speed/interval work
- **Thu:** Rest, yoga, or easy session
- **Fri (day off):** Big training day — extended gym: vert treadmill + strength + stairmaster
- **Fri (working):** Gym evening: strength B + treadmill incline, or lunch run + evening gym
- **Sat:** Long run (morning)
- **Sun:** Easy run / back-to-back (from week 4)

**Intensity distribution:** Hard days = Wed (track) + Sat (long). Moderate = Tue (gym), Fri (vert). Easy = Mon, Thu, Sun.

## KEY PATTERNS

- **Tue + Fri = gym days** (strength, treadmill, stairmaster) as preferred
- **Wed = track session** with local team (default every week; dial back in taper, optional in sharpening)
- **Free Fridays (Apr 24, May 8, May 22, Jun 5, Jun 19)** = big training days with extended gym sessions
- **Lunch runs** on Mon/Thu/Fri when 12:00-13:30 is clear
- **Long runs** always Saturday morning
- **Back-to-backs** Sat+Sun from Week 4 onward
- **Ardennes weekends** targeting Weeks 5 and 7 (May 16-17 and May 30-31)
- **No early mornings** — all sessions fit lunch, afternoon, evening, or weekend morning slots
